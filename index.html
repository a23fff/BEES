Introduction to Bees

Bees are flying insects closely related to wasps and ants. They are known for their important role in pollinating plants and producing honey. Wild bees species live on every continent except Antarctica. Bees feed exclusively on sugary nectar and protein-rich pollen from flowering plants, unlike the carnivorous wasps from which they evolved.
